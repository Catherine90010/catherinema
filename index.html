To Be Brief...

I finished my doctoral program at the University of Miami (UM), focusing on visualization communication research. I studied the shared mental model in news visualization production for my dissertation.

Before my Ph.D study, I was a graphics reporter working for the Xinhua News Agency, producing news visualization stories in various topics and formats.

I hope to develop a hybrid-profil career in the visualization field, working as a research-based full-stack data visualization designer.

I'm also a certificated English-Chinese interpreter, always wanting to bridge the two beautiful languages and the underpinned cultures.

At my leisure, I read books, play the piano, and do sketches. I am also a Taekwondo athlete with Kukkiwon Black Belt.
